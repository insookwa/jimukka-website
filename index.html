More about us?

Dedicated to quality healthy food production

Jimukka Grain Milling Solutions will be a renowned company, focusing on the production of high-quality maize flour. Situated in Busoke-Kakoni center 5km off Zirobwe road, Luwero Uganda, our company will pride itself on its commitment to delivering premium products to meet the food and nutrition needs of our customers especially low-income earners using environmental sustainability practices. With a team of experts and experienced people and modern production methods, Jimukka will stand as a leading name in the maize milling sector.

Established by the director Mr. Ivan Ssembogga who has worked in the manufacturing industry for 11 years along with the shareholders Mrs. Jesca Ssembogga and Mr. Geofrey Ssebagala, the company is registered by URSB and licensed by NEMA and currently working to obtain a Trading Licensee in accordance with the regulations set forth by the relevant authorities..

At Jimukka, we will specialize in the Maize processing, milling of maize flour and Maize bran (Animal feeds), while adhering to stringent quality standards through more products that support in fighting malnutrition will also be developed as the company expands. Our maize milling process will begin with carefully selected inputs sourced from the company farms and a few trusted suppliers and cooperatives, ensuring the finest quality raw materials for our production.

Currently the company owns its own acre of land, locally purchased Milling machines, constructed a 3-phase power line with 100kv transformer a finished slab of Milling and Animal Feeds warehouses and wishes to expand on the same in order to utilize part of this land to establish a factory complex of maize milling system, maize brand and Animal feeds storage, a cleaning, drying and grading system and silo storage facility. It will purchase and install maize milling system in a ware house that will be constructed for that purpose. The company will also install a cleaning, drying and grading system for maize trading in second warehouse and third warehouse for maize bran storage. The three systems will need supply of large quantities of maize grain hence the company will construct 6* 1500 MT silos.

Our market will extend beyond local boundaries, reaching both domestic and international market. We will take pride in our ability to meet the demands of diverse markets, offering premium maize flour products that meet and exceed customer expectations.
